Cyber Ethics

Responsible Digital Behaviour for a Safer Internet

What is Cyber Ethics?

Cyber ethics is a set of moral principles for using technology responsibly. It encourages fair, respectful, and secure online conduct, ensuring that the internet is a place for learning, communication, and innovation without harm.

Why is Cyber Ethics Important?

With our dependence on the internet growing every day, ethical online behaviour is critical to protect individuals and communities. Without it, we risk increased cyber threats, misinformation, and privacy violations.

Key Benefits of Practising Cyber Ethics

  • Secures your personal and financial information
  • Builds a positive and trustworthy online identity
  • Reduces risks from hackers and online fraud
  • Encourages respectful and healthy digital communities

If you face any cyber crime in India, you can:

  1. Visit the official National Cyber Crime Reporting Portal
  2. Call your local police helpline
  3. Provide screenshots, emails, and any evidence

Case Study: Online Scam Incident

In 2023, a fake job portal tricked thousands into paying registration fees for non-existent jobs. The criminals were tracked through digital payment records and prosecuted under the IT Act. This case highlights the importance of verifying sources before making payments online.
